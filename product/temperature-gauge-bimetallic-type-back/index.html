Temperature Gauge Bimetallic Type Back

The Bimetal thermometer employs a bimetal strip in the form of the helix (it works on the principle of thermal expansion - two metals having different coefficients of expansions are joined to form a bimetal. The resultant expansion of bimetal is proper.

 

Applications:

Food & beverage, pharmaceutical, cryogenics, chemical & petrochemical industries, conventional & nuclear power plants, pumps, hydro-cleaning machines, presses, engine compressors, turbines, diesel engines & refrigerating plants.

 

Special Features:

  • Stainless steel case & stem
  • Bottom / Back / Every Angle Entry
  • Standard Followed: EN 13190
